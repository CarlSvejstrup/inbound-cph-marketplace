# About Inbound CPH

Inbound CPH is an established digital marketing agency delivering data-driven, results-oriented solutions to a B2B client portfolio. The agency acts as both strategic partner and execution arm for companies looking to strengthen their online presence and maximise digital ROI.

|---|---|---|---|
| **SEM** (Search Engine Marketing) | 4 | 50 | Google Ads, Meta Ads, Supermetrics, Looker Studio, GA4, Search Console |
| **SEO & Linkbuilding** | 7 | 80 | Ahrefs, SEO Monitor, Supermetrics, GA4, Search Console |
| **Social Media & Content** | 4 | cross-cutting | Meta ads platform, Looker Studio, GA4 |
| **Tracking & Analyse** | 2 | cross-cutting | Looker Studio, Supermetrics, GTM |

Strong Nordic-language and cultural-relevance focus. Quality and craft pride run high.

## Core service areas

1. **SEM** — paid search and social, conversion optimisation, ROAS focus
2. **SEO & Linkbuilding** — technical SEO, content, domain authority
3. **Social Media & Content** — engagement, community, organic social
4. **Tracking & Analyse** — measurement foundation for all the above

## Strategic position

The agency is at an inflection point. The traditional model (linear: more clients = more hires) is being challenged by AI. The strategic move is to AI-enable the workflow stack, freeing up to ~40% of capacity currently spent on routine operational work (manual reporting, data collection, ad-copy rewrites, repetitive SEO drafting, spend monitoring).

The freed capacity becomes optionality: scale the client base aggressively with the same headcount, or consolidate for higher margin per client.

The Kunde Specialist agent (this plugin) is part of that transformation. It moves specialists from "producers" to "editors and strategists" by handling the synthesis, monitoring, and drafting layer, under the human-in-the-loop write contract.

## What this means for an agent running here

- The clients are B2B, mostly Nordic, mostly performance-marketing-led.
- Quality bar is high; Nordic restraint beats hype every time.
- The team values craft. Do not ship slop, even if the user approves it, push back if a draft is weak.
- Time saved is the unit of value. If the skill takes longer than the user would have, it is failing.
